FAQs

Quick answers to your questions

Answers to questions you may have. We're here to help you make an informed decision.

1. What is included with my purchase?

With every purchase of FinPath, you will get:

  • A lifetime license to use the product.
  • Access to import any of the starter sites showcased above.
  • KeyDesign Framework and third-party plugins.
  • Access to our exclusive 600+ Template Library blocks.
  • 6 months of free customer support that can be extended if needed.
  • Access to all future updates without any extra cost.
2. Do I need additional plugins to create websites?

Our themes come equipped with all the necessary tools to create a fully functional website from scratch. You can also use any of our pre-built starter sites as a starting point for your project. If you require advanced features beyond what our themes offer, you have the flexibility to install any type of plugin. In case any compatibility issues arise, our support team will be available to assist you.

3. Are all the starter sites included and customizable?
Absolutely! With every purchase of the FinPath, you get access to our entire collection of existing and future prebuilt starter sites. Once you import a starter site, you have complete freedom to customize its content and layouts to match your project specifications. Whether it's changing text, colors, or layouts, you can easily tailor the site using the intuitive Elementor Page Builder.
4. How can I get support after purchasing?

We offer dedicated support through our forum, where a team of knowledgeable agents is ready to assist you. You can find the support forum here. Additionally, we recommend consulting our theme documentation to find answers to common questions.

5. Why should I choose KeyDesign Themes?

With us, you can count on getting expert support and reliable WordPress themes with life lasting updates. Here are 4 reasons why you should choose us:

  • We’re an Envato Elite Author with over 27,000 sales.
  • We’ve been selling on ThemeForest for the past 9 Years.
  • Our WordPress Themes rate an average of 4,5 stars.
  • Top-notch customer support with every purchase.
6. Where can I buy a license of FinPath?

FinPath can be exclusively purchased through Envato Market (ThemeForest). Please avoid downloading from unauthorized sources or purchasing from other platforms, as it is both illegal and potentially unsafe.

7. Can I use the theme for multiple websites?

According to ThemeForest's license policy, one license is valid for one project, which includes both development and production sites. If you plan to create multiple websites using our theme, you'll need to acquire a regular license for each individual domain or website.

8. How do I get updates after the purchase?

You will get update notifications for your plugins/themes in your WordPress dashboard the same as any other theme or plugin. Please note you will require an active license to access the updates.

9. Can I use stock images from your starter sites?
Yes, you can. All images provided on our pre-built websites are stock-free and can be used on any commercial or personal site without any restrictions.
10. Can I trial test the theme before I purchase it?
Unfortunately, trial testing is not available. If you want to take a closer look under the hood, the theme documentation includes screenshots and detailed info. If you have any pre-sale questions please use the comments area or the live chat widget.
